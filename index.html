Courses Offered

Course packages available for students.

Full Syllabi Course

This includes all the services offered by the classes for a particular standard, which includes regular tests (online), in-depth explanation of topics, individual focus, doubt-clearing sessions and feedback. This course gives you exclusive access to performance stats and analysis of the student.

Subject Specific Course

This course focuses on a particular subject the student might be interested in. Ideal for students who need special attention in certain subjects or topics.

Crash Course

This is ideal for students who missed the deadline to join the regular courses. This course is fast-paced, focuses more on problem-solving and frequently asked exam concepts.
